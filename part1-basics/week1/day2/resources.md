# Resources

## Essential Reading

### OWASP Top 10 for LLM Applications
**URL**: https://owasp.org/www-project-top-10-for-large-language-model-applications/

The definitive guide to security risks when using AI tools. Focus on:
- LLM06: Sensitive Information Disclosure
- LLM09: Overreliance
- LLM02: Insecure Output Handling

**Why read**: Understand security vulnerabilities specific to AI coding tools.

---

### GitHub Copilot Trust Center
**URL**: https://resources.github.com/copilot-trust-center/

Everything about how Copilot handles your code:
- Data privacy policies
- Training data usage
- Enterprise vs individual plans
- Duplicate code detection

**Why read**: Know how your code is processed and stored.

---

### Anthropic's Responsible Scaling Policy
**URL**: https://www.anthropic.com/news/anthropics-responsible-scaling-policy

How AI companies think about safety and responsible development.

**Why read**: Understand AI safety thinking from a leading AI company.

---

### IEEE Guidelines on AI Ethics
**URL**: https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10983504

Professional standards for ethical AI development.

**Why read**: Framework for ethical decision-making with AI.

*Note: May require IEEE membership or institutional access.*

## Quick Reference

**Before using AI, read**:
1. OWASP LLM Top 10 (security)
2. Your tool's trust center (data policy)
3. Your org's AI policy (if exists)

**When reviewing AI code, check**:
1. OWASP Top 25 (vulnerabilities)
2. WCAG guidelines (accessibility)
3. Your judgment (bias, assumptions)
